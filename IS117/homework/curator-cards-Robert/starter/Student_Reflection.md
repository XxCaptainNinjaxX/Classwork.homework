# Design Curator Cards: Student Reflection

This assignment fundamentally changed how I approach AI. In the past, I would use a "zero-shot" prompt and just ask the AI to do the whole assignment at once, which usually resulted in generic, hallucinated text. By forcing myself to do the "Explore -> Pin -> Promote" workflow, the output was incredibly sharp and required almost no editing.

The most valuable part was the Expert Critique. When I asked the AI to act as David Carson to critique my Grunge Typography card, it challenged my assumption that Grunge was just "random." It pointed out that the chaos was highly intentional and intuitive, focused on emotional resonance rather than mathematical grids. This forced me to revise my draft and find better evidence. I realized that my job is no longer just typing; it is acting as the editor and verifying that the AI isn't just making things sound good, but that they are actually true.
